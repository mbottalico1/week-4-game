Crystal Collector



At the start of this game, the computer will generate a random number between 19-120.

When you click on a crystal it will generate a random number, when you click on another
crystal that will also generate a random number and add it to the previous updated number.

The goal of this game is to match your number with the computer generated number.
If you match the number, YOU WIN! If you go over that number then YOU LOSE!
